Who I am

I'm a Data Scientist / AI Engineer who got into the field by building predictive models in healthcare — figuring out which patients would benefit most from social care interventions before outcomes got worse. That work taught me to care about building things that actually ship and actually help people.


Now I spend most of my time on retrieval systems, LLM tooling, and generative models. I built augur, an open-source TypeScript package that intelligently routes RAG queries to the optimal retrieval strategy — vector, BM25, hybrid, or reranking — instead of committing to a single fixed approach. I founded and scaled Pollenate, a platform improving commercial pollination for growers and pollinators. I also run an AI-generated music channel on YouTube — every track and video is fully produced by an automated pipeline I built from scratch.
